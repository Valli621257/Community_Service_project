# Community_Service_project
This project focuses on Awareness of Education in Kommarigiripatnam village. It includes socio-economic survey, identification of educational problems, and conducting awareness programmes to encourage students and parents about the importance of education.

# Community Service Project (CSP)  
# Awareness of Education

# Project Title
**Awareness of Education in Kommarigiripatnam Village**

# Objective
The main objective of this Community Service Project is to create awareness among rural people about the importance of education and to encourage students to continue their studies without dropping out.


# About the Village
The project was conducted in **Kommarigiripatnam habitation**. Most people in the village depend on agriculture and daily wage work. The village has basic educational facilities, but awareness about higher education is still limited.


# Work Done
- Conducted socio-economic survey in the village  
- Collected data using questionnaires  
- Interacted with parents and students  
- Conducted awareness programmes on education  
- Explained government education schemes  
- Motivated school dropouts to continue studies  


# Problems Identified
- Lack of awareness about higher education  
- Financial difficulties  
- School dropouts after secondary education  
- Limited knowledge of government schemes  
- Lack of career guidance  


#  Outcomes
- Increased awareness about importance of education  
- Some dropouts showed interest in continuing studies  
- Parents understood benefits of education  
- Improved knowledge about government schemes  


# Conclusion
Education is very important for the development of individuals and society. Awareness programmes helped villagers understand the value of education and encouraged students to continue their studies for a better future.
